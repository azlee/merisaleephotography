FAQ


What type of photography do you do?

I focus on digital photography with a passion for capturing the beauty and emotions of people. I love to photograph weddings, couples, headshots and families.

Expect to have fun if doing a photoshoot with me! I understand that not everyone is comfortable in front of a camera (myself included!) however I try to make people feel at ease and I have silly cues in hopes to get natural smiles out of you :)

What are your rates?

I charge $100/hour for portraits and weddings for $1000 (includes a complimentary engagement shoot).

How many photos can I expect?

I focus on quality over quantity however you can expect around 80 edited photos an hour.

How long do I have to wait for photos?

Portrait photos will take around 3 weeks and wedding photos around 10 weeks.

When are you available?

Please send me an email at leemerisa@gmail.com with some information on what kind of pictures you are looking for along with details (ie where, when, what)! I'd be happy to chat and accommodate your needs!
