FAQ


What type of photography do you do?

I focus on digital photography with a passion for capturing the beauty and emotions of people. I love to photograph weddings, couples, families and portraits. I would describe my photography as colorful and fun.

What can I expect with a photoshoot with you?

Expect to have fun! I totally understand that not everyone is comfortable in front of a camera (myself included!) however I do my best to make people feel at ease and want your photos to allow you to relive fun memories. I have fun cues in hopes to get natural smiles out of you :)

How many photoshoots can I expect?

I focus on quality over quantity however you can expect around 100 photos an hour.

When are you available?

Please send me an email at leemerisa@gmail.com with some information on what kind of pictures you are looking for! I'd be happy to chat and accommodate your needs!
